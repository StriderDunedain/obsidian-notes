> [!abstract] Definition
> If a signal $x(t)$ has a fundamental period $t_{0}$, then its **fundamental frequency** us defined as the reciprocal of $t_{0}$
> $$
> f_{0} = \frac{1}{t_{0}}
> $$

If the period is infinity, then $f_{0} = 0$, meaning that it makes 0 cycles a second (or any number of seconds, for that matter)
