Everything-as-a-Service:

- ##### IaaS – Infrastructure-as-a-Service
	You rent computer resources: VMs, storage, networks. You manage the OS and applications and the supplier the hardware

- ##### PaaS – Platform-as-a-Service
	You get ready-made dev envs: runtime envs, DBs, middleware. You manage the code, the supplier the infrastructure and the platform

- ##### SaaS – Software-as-a-Service
	You use full applications: emails, CRMs, microslop office

Others include: Function-, Data-, DB-, Network-, Business Process-as-a-Service
