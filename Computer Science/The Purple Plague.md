A mechanical (or, rather, chemical) failure that happens when gold and aluminum are welded together. Since gold amalgamates with aluminum a lot faster than the other way around, it created *gold dialuminide* ($AuAl_{2}$), or simply *purple gold*, which is very brittle and has different properties. On top of that is also creates Kirkendall voids: spaces where there's nothing
