After discovering that real numbers' cardinality is:
$$|\mathbb{R}| = 2^{\aleph_{0}}$$
He had a question: Is there a cardinality such that:
$$\aleph_{0} < |A| < |\mathbb{R}|$$
Kurt Gödel and Paul Cohen later proved that the hypothesis can be neither proved nor disproved using the standard axioms showing that a system can't prove its own axioms
