### Direct proof (direkte Beweis)
This is the type of proof where it is shown that A => B (i.e. that A -> B is always true)

### Equivalence proof (Äquivalenzbeweis)
This is proof by equivalence <=> (i.e. that A => B and B => A)

### Proof by contradiction (Widerspruchsbeweis)
Exactly a it sounds, this is proof by saying not that A => B but not B => not A or tautology. One can prove that the square root of 2 isn't part of N as well as there are infinitely many prime numbers. Essentially you take the opposite of what you need to prove and prove that that is bs or something else

Look to [[Modus Ponens]] and [[Modus Tollens]], too

An interesting base case is the [[Vacuous Truth]]
