Exclusive Disjunction, or XOR, to natural language translates to **either P or Q**:
$$ \oplus,\ \nleftrightarrow,\ \veebar\ or\  \not\equiv $$
Can be expanded as:
$$
(P \land \neg Q) \lor (\neg P \land Q)
$$
