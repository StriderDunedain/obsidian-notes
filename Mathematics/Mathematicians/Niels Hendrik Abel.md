A Norwegian mathematician (5 Aug #history/1802; 6 Apr #history/1829) that invented the concept of [[Group]]s that was, as it often is, overlooked for a long time. Although he hasn't lived long, he is considered one of the most influential mathematicians of the 19th century

### Abel Prise
The Norwegian government later founded the Abel Prise which is considered to be the Noble Prise equivalent (since the latter doesn't give out awards for math). First given out in #history/2003
