Enantiomorphs are mirror images that cannot be superimposed using only rotations and translations. Like hands. They are mirror images but you would have to make an exceptional move to make them align. Not by simply rotating

Not quite sure they are supposed to be in morphisms but let them sit here for now. Closely related concept is [[Chirality]]. Used in crystallography
