Dual spaces built from [[Vector Space]]s like $V = \mathbb{R}^{n}$. Denoted as $V^{*}$, it is a set of all [[Linear Map]]s:
$$
f: V \to  \mathbb{R}
$$
These maps take vectors and return numbers. They are called linear functionals or [[Covector]]s
