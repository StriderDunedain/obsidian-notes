### Gaussian elimination vs GJ
The difference between Gauss-Jordan and [[Gaussian Elimination]] lies in where it takes the solution. For most cases the normal elimination suffices but if you want complete control of the structure:
- You see which variables are basic and which are free
- You see how many solutions a system has
