> [!summary] Definition
> A [[Matrix]] is considered orthogonal if:
> $$
> A^{T}A = I 
> $$
> i.e. that $A^{-1} = A^{T}$

Such transformations preserve lengths, angles and volumes. Examples include rotations and reflections
***
For more info: [[Transposed Matrix]]
