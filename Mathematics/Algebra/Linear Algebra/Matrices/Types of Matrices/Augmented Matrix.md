An augmented [[Matrix]] is a system of linear equations' coefficients and constants:
$$
\begin{cases}
x + 2y = 5 \\
3x - y = 4
\end{cases}
\to
\left[
\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 2 & 5 \\
3 & -1 & 4
\end{array}
\right]
$$
This isn't a fundamentally new matrix, just a handy notation for, e.g., [[Gaussian Elimination]]
