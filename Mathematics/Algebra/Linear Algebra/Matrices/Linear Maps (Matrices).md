> [!summary] Definition
> For every [[Linear Map]] $f: K^{n} \to K^{m}$ there exists just one matrix $A \in K^{m \times n}$ with the property $f(x) = Ax$ for all $x \in K^{n}$



***
Matrices and linear maps are basically the same thing in $\mathbb{R}^{2 \times 2}$
