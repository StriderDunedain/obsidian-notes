> [!abstract] Definition
> Let $G$ be a [[Group]] and $N \leq G$ be a [[Normal Subgroup]] then the quotient group (Faktorgruppe) is the set of the left [[Coset]]s:
> $$
> G / N = \{ gN : g \in G \}
> $$
> The group operation is defined by:
> $$
> (gN)(hN) = (gh)N
> $$

The normality of $N$ guarantees that this operation is well-defined
