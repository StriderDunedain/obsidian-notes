> [!abstract] Definition
> Let $G$ be a [[Group]] and $N \leq G$ a subgroup. Then $N$ is called the normal subgroup (Normalteiler) if:
> $$
> gN = Ng, \forall g \in G
> $$
> Equivalently,
> $$
> gNg^{-1} = N, \forall g \in G
> $$

Interestingly, an abelian subgroup is automatically a normal subgroup, too, since both of them have to be commutative
