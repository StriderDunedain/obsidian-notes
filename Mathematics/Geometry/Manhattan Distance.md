Also called the taxicab geometry or city-block distance is the distance between 2 points on a grid by summing the absolute difference between each point's relative coordinates . The general formula is:
$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n}  |p_{i} - q_{i}|
$$
For the 2D space it simplifies down to
$$
\text{MD} = |x_{1} - x_{2}| + |y_{1} - y_{2}|
$$
