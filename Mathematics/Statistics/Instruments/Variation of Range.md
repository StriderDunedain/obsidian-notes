> [!summary]
> Answers: "How big is the range relative to average?"

Also called *coefficient of oscillation* (коэффицент осцилляции) shows how large the [[Range]] is relative to the [[Mean]]
$$
K_{0} = \frac{x_{max} - x_{min}}{\overline{x}}
$$
