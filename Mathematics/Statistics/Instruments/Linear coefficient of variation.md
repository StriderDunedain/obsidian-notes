> Answers: "How large the MAD is compared to the mean?"

(also called линейный коэффицент вариации) measures the [[Mean Absolute Deviation]]'s relation to the [[Mean]] in percentages:

$$
K_{linear} = \frac{MAD}{\overline{x}} \cdot 100\%
$$
What this means is that is establishes a relationship between the "averageness" of deviation (see [[Absolute Deviation]]) and the average of the set and asks how close are they to each other?
