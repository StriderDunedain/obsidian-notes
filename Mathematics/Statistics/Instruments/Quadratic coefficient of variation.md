> Answers: "How large the SD is compared to the mean?"

Also for simplicity just *coefficient of variation* (Квадратичный коэффициент вариации) measures the same thing as the [[Linear coefficient of variation]] but if that one uses [[Mean Absolute Deviation]] this relies on the [[Standard Deviation]]:
$$
V_{\sigma} = \frac{\sigma}{\overline{x}} \cdot 100\%
$$
