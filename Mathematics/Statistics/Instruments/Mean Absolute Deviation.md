> Answers: "How average is absolute deviation?"

Mean absolute deviation, or $MAD$, – среднее линейное отклонение – measures the "averageness" of the [[Absolute Deviation]]
$$
MAD = \frac{\sum |x - \overline{x}|}{N}
$$
When dealing with frequencies, you can also just add it to the equation:
$$
MAD = \frac{ \sum (|x_{i} - \overline{x}| \cdot f_{i})}{\sum f_{i}}
$$
However, $\sum f_{i}$ is the same as $N$
