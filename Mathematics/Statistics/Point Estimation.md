Called точечная оценка, this method, unlike [[Interval Estimation]], tries to estimate the parameters of the population by saying that a parameter's value of a sample is kinda equal to the same parameter's value of the population:
$$
\overline{x}_{sample} = 13 \implies \overline{x}_{population} \approx 13
$$
