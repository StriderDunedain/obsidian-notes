*Obelus* is the $\div$ and the gradient is nabla $\nabla$ ($\nabla \times$ is the curl and $\nabla \cdot$ is the divergence)

The bar in the fraction $\frac{a}{b}$ is called the *vinculum* and it's also used to indicate fractions в периоде: $0.\overline{3}$ and the "slash" in the same division is called the *solidus*

The reciprocal / Kehrwert / обратная величина / аликвотная дробь is a fraction that looks like $\frac{1}{x}$
