- Gaussian Elimination shortcut:
$$
\left(
\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6
\end{array}
\right)
$$
- To make a separator "such that", use \mid:
$$
\{ x \mid x \in \mathbb{R}\}
$$
- To make a big "|" to the right of the matrix, use \left. to indicate an empty delimiter and the \right|
$$
\left.
\begin{matrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{matrix}
\right|
$$
