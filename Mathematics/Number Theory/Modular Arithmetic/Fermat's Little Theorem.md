> [!summary] Definition
> Let $p$ be a prime number coprime with an integer $a$ (i.e. $\text{gcd}(a, p) = 1$) then:
> $$
> a^{p - 1} \equiv 1 \quad \text{ mod } p
> $$

This is a special case of [[Euler's Theorem]]
