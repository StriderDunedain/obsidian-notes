1. **Addition:**
$$n + (m + 1) := (n + m) + 1$$
For addition the operands are: addend and the result is sum

2. **Multiplication:**
$$n * 1 = n$$
$$P(0) \land (\forall n,(P(n) \Rightarrow P(S(n)))) \Rightarrow \forall n,P(n)$$
For multiplication the operands are: factor and the result is product

For subtraction the operands are: minuend and subtrahend and the result is difference

For division the operands are: dividend and divisor and the result is quotient
