Dates back to #history/1742 and is known to hold up to numbers up to $10^{18}$ but nobody really knows its veracity. It's formulated as:
> [!quote]
> Every integer greater than 2 is the sum of two primes

Or, in LaTeX:
$$
\forall n \in \mathbb{Z}, \ n > 2, \ (2 | n), \implies \exists p, q \in \mathbb{P}, \ n = p + q
$$
