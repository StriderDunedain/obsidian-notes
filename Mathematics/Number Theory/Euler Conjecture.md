Formulated by Euler in #history/1769 it goes like this:
$$
\forall a, b, c, d, \in \mathbb{Z}^{+}, \ a^{4} + b^{4} + c^{4} \neq d^{4}
$$

Was proved to be false 218 years later in #history/1987 by Noam Elkies at a liberal arts school
