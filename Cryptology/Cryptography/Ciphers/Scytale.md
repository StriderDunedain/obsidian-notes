An ancient type of a [[Transposition Cipher]] that involves writing a message on a long and narrow parchment wrapped around a cylinder so that when unwound it'd be incoherent gibberish. Very easy to crack, ofc – just find a cylinder of a proportionate size

The hardest part of this is honestly the pronunciation. It's either */ˈskɪtəliː/* or */ˈskaɪteɪl/*
