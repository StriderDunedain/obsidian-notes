These types of ciphers use a certain keyword to substitute the alphabet. Really easier to show than to explain. Say, the keyword were **CRYPTANLS**:

| Cleartext:  | a   | b   | c   | d   | e     | f     | g     | h     | i     | j     | k     | l     | m     | n   | o   | p   | q   | r   | s   | t   | u   | v   | x   | y   | z   |
| ----------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ciphertext: | V   | W   | X   | Z   | **C** | **R** | **I** | **P** | **T** | **A** | **N** | **L** | **S** | I   | B   | D   | E   | F   | G   | H   | J   | K   | M   | O   | Q   |
