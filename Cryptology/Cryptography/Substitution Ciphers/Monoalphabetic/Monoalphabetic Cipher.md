Encompasses all ciphers that use just one language. And since all natural languages have a non-uniform character distribution, they are very easy to break (by [[Statistical Analysis]], for example). That's why there exist non-natural alphabets or [[Polyalphabetic Cipher]]s

One big example of a monoalphabetic cipher is [[DES]]
